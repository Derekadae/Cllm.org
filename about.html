About Us

Christian Light of Life Ministry is a Bible-believing, Christ-centered church dedicated to reaching the world with the gospel of Jesus Christ.

Our Mission

To bring the light and life of Jesus Christ to individuals, families, and communities through worship, teaching, and service.

Our Vision

To raise a generation that walks in the light of God’s word, empowered by the Holy Spirit to live purposefully and serve faithfully.

Leadership

Our church is led by a team of devoted pastors, ministers, and elders committed to shepherding the congregation in love and truth.

Our History

Founded in 2018, Christian Light of Life Ministry has grown into a vibrant, multi-generational church community. We are grateful for God’s faithfulness and the lives being transformed through His power.
